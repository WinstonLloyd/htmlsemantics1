About Myself

  • Winston Lloyd Timogan
  • November 7, 2003
  • Culit Nasipit Agusan Del Norte

I'm Winston Lloyd Timogan, a dedicated student by day, delves into the captivating world of Mobile Legends by night. With textbooks replaced by strategy guides, he navigates the complexities of both academia and the virtual battlefield. In class, he daydreams of perfecting his hero skills, and during breaks, he passionately discusses game strategies with fellow enthusiasts. His backpack bears the subtle marks of a gamer's lifestyle a mobile charger, snacks for sustained energy during intense matches, and a notebook filled with hero sketches. For Winston, the corridors of education and the realms of Mobile Legends seamlessly intertwine, creating a unique narrative of a student who aspires to be a hero in more ways than one.
